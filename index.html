Steps to add Naxa Email Signature in Gmail

  1. Copy the email signature
  2. Open https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#settings/general from company email
  3. On signature textbox, paste the signature and update your Name, Role & Phone Number
  4. Press Save Changes in Button
